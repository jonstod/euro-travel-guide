About EuroGuide:


EuroGuide is a student-developed web application developed to provide a general overview of all United Nations members and observers in Europe. On each page, you will find general information pertaining to the respective country, sourced from the country's Wikipedia page, a popular subreddit (a community hosted on Reddit), and the United States Federal Reserve. This information includes the general information about a country listed on its Wikipedia page, the top 10 upvoted posts of all-time on a country's selected subreddit, and the conversion rate between a country's official currency and the U.S. Dollar when available.


I hope that you will find this resource useful, whether you are using it to plan your next trip, brainstorm travel ideas, do some simple research, or even just to pass by to take a peek at my work. Whichever it may be - thanks for looking, happy travels, and Go Beavs!
